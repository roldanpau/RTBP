Detailed Description

Continuation of the periodic orbit wrt energy H.

Consider the family of p:q almost-resonant periodic orbits in the RTBP. Compute the periodic orbit for a given (wide) range of energies when we change the energy H.

Remarks:
The initial condition for the periodic orbit is taken at the perihelion.
