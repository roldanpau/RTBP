Detailed Description

Take a point $p$ on section S1 to section S2.

Consider the RTBP. Let S1 be the Poincare section {y=0, vy>0}. Let S2 be the Poincare section {y=0, vy<0}. Fix the Hamiltonian to a given value "H". Using this energy condition, we can work with only two variables, $(x,p_x)$. The third variable $y$ is 0 since we look at the Poincare section, and the fourth variable $p_y$ can be obtained from the energy condition. These functions take a point $p$ on section S1 to section S2 by either forward or backward integration.
