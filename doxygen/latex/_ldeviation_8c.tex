Given a periodic orbit $\gamma_J(t)$ of the RTBP in rotating Delaunay coordinates \$(l,L,g,G)\$, compute the maximum deviation of the \$L\$ coordinate (sqrt of semi-\/major axis) from that of the true resonant periodic orbit, i.e. compute \[ max{|L(t)-L^*|} \] for all $t \in [0,T_J]$, where \$L$^\wedge$$\ast$ = 3$^\wedge$\{-\/1/3\}\$ for the 3:1 resonance.

\hline
