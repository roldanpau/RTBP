Computes the vectorfield of the reduced RTBP problem.

The function computes the vectorfield of the reduced RTBP at a point named \char`\"{}x\char`\"{}. Specifically, we use the equations of motion of the reduced planar circular RTBP in Delaunay coordinates (l,L,g,G,t,I), in the rotating (synodic) coordinate system.


\begin{DoxyParams}[1]{Parameters}
\mbox{\tt in}  & {\em s} & adimensional time at which the vectorfield is evaluated. Since this is an autonomous ODE (does not depend on time), this parameter is not used.\\
\hline
\mbox{\tt in}  & {\em x\mbox{[}6\mbox{]}} & point in phase space, 6 coordinates: (l,L,g,G,t,I).\\
\hline
\mbox{\tt out}  & {\em y\mbox{[}6\mbox{]}} & vectorfield at (s,x), 6 coordinates: d/ds(l,L,g,G,t,I).\\
\hline
\mbox{\tt in}  & {\em params} & pointer to the parameter of the system: the mass ratio \char`\"{}mu\char`\"{}.\\
