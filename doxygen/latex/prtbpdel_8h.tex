Poincare map of RTBP in Delaunay coordinates

Consider the RTBP in rotating coordinates. Let S be the Poincare section SEC1 or SEC2, corresponding to \{l=0\} or $\{l=\pi\}$. Let \$x\$ be a point in the section, and suppose that flow at a point \$x\$ is transversal to the section. Compute the n-\/th iterate of the Poincare map \$P$^\wedge$n(x)\$ of the RTBP. This procedure also computes, as a side product, the integration time to intersect the Poincare section \char`\"{}n\char`\"{} times.

\end{DoxyParams}

\end{DoxyReturn}
\begin{DoxyRemark}{Remarks}
To compute the inverse Poincare map (i.e., negative iterates of the Poincare map), see \hyperlink{prtbpdel_8h_aa2e8335cb7438ea7702d4e2d24832dfd}{prtbp\_\-del\_\-inv}.

On successful return of this function, the point \$x\$ is exactly on the section, i.e. we set coordinate \$l\$ exactly equal to zero/pi.

Since \$g\$ is an angle, we normalize it to $[0,2\pi)$. 
\end{DoxyRemark}
