Flow of the RTBP in Delaunay coords: main prog.

Compute the flow $(t,x)$ of the RTBP for a given time and initial condition. The time $t$ may be positive or negative, allowing for forward or backward integration. The trajectory is integrated numerically.

It reads the following input from stdin:

  • mu: RTBP mass parameter
  • t1: integration time
  • x[DIM]: initial condition, x=(l,L,g,G)

The final point is written to stdout, in the form x=(l,L,g,G)
